**Security Group**

**Group-Based Licensing**  
Method of assigning licenses to users based on group membership instead of individual assignment.

**Microsoft 365 Admin Center**  
Portal used to manage licenses, subscriptions, and Microsoft 365 services.

**Incident (INC)**  
A support ticket was created in response to a user-facing issue.

**Propagation Delay**  
The time required for changes (such as licensing or group membership) to apply across services.
